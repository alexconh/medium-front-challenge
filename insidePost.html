Well, you’re in luck! Today, I will be running down a list of the top 5 easiest ways to make money using your computer. Maybe we’ll do a top 10 in the future? For now it’s top 5 tips for 2022. I’m talking about the gig economy here. It’s a great way for people to make money and earn a steady and passive income — all in the comfort of your own home. These sites only require that you sign up, with some offering premium subscriptions to boost sales and grant access to features like bidding on more proposals.
